INT 21h AH=2AH Get date (DOS 1.0+)
────────────────────────────────────────────

Input:
    AH = 2Ah

Output:
    Return: AL = day of the week (0=Sunday)
	    CX = year (1980-2099)
	    DH = month (1-12)
	    DL = day (1-31)

Action:
    Get date.
    

Notes:
    Date based on DOS maintained clock.
    Update BIOS Data Area at location 40:70.
